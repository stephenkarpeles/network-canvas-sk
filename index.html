Network Canvas is a set of apps for surveying personal networks

We have taken an innovative approach to capturing network data digitally, using modern web technologies and touch-optimized interfaces. To facilitate ease of use, the software's design is colorful, tactile, and intuitive for both researchers and participants.

Our goal is to build a suite of tools for the research community that is high quality, free, safe for research, and built to last.

An end-to-end workflow for your research

Network Canvas has a tool for survey design, a tool for interviewing, and a tool for secure case management.

Through these three components – Architect, Server, and the Network Canvas application itself – social and behavioral researchers can easily design, capture, and export data on both their participants and the people and places with whom their participants interact.
